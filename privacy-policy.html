1. Service Overview

Pingbix operates as a Communication Platform as a Service (CPaaS), providing integrated solutions for businesses to communicate effectively with their users across multiple digital channels.

2. Meta Platform Usage

Pingbix utilizes Meta platforms, including Facebook, Instagram, and WhatsApp APIs, to enable and facilitate comprehensive business messaging services. By using our platform, you acknowledge that communications are processed through these third-party Meta infrastructures to ensure delivery.

3. Data Collection & Processing

We collect and process data only as strictly required to provide our messaging services. This data includes:

  • Message Content: The body of communications sent via our platform.
  • Metadata: Information related to the message delivery, such as timestamps and status.
  • Account Identifiers: Specific IDs required to route messages to the correct recipients.

4. Data Usage & Protection

We do not sell personal data. Your data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Efficient service delivery.
  • Regulatory and legal compliance.
  • Platform security and fraud prevention.

5. User Rights & Data Deletion

Users maintain full rights over their personal information. You may request access to your data or request the permanent deletion of your data at any time.

To exercise these rights, please contact our privacy team at: whatsapp@pingbix.com

6. Policy Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to all Pingbix applications, software integrations, and CPaaS offerings across all geographical regions.
